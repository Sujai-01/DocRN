01Mar 2024
Short-Term Outcomes of Oesophagectomy in a Real-World Scenario from a Tier II City in India
Rigved Nittala et al.
Indian Journal of Surgical Oncology · Springer Nature · doi: 10.1007/s13193-024-01924-y

Evaluates short-term outcomes of oesophagectomy at a low-volume cancer hospital in Visakhapatnam, India. 15 patients (2020–2023). No perioperative deaths. Demonstrated that proper patient selection and a collaborative team approach can achieve acceptable results.

02Dec 2025
Textbook Outcomes in Oesophageal Resections from an Evolving Thoracic Surgical Unit
Vishnu Santhosh Menon, Rigved Nittala, Sekhar Padhy, Rajendrasingh Arora, Mounika Basani
Peer-Reviewed Surgical Journal

Textbook Outcome (TO) is a composite metric of surgical quality. Among 41 patients, TO was achieved in 14 (34.1%). Optimal lymph node yield (≥15 nodes) was the most challenging parameter (25/41, 61%).
